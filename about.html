What We Do

We made Robotic arm system that will help you drawing real life pixelart.

We make your dreams of becoming an artist come true! Just choose your pixel and you're ready to go! When the painting ends we sell it on charity auction and share money to charity/volunteer organizations who fight against COVID-19. Then we put a new painting for you to draw, that is unique, because it is made by people all over the world, and exactly you can become owner of this one and only painting.
